## Structure-Based Virtual Screening

Software:
- Schrödinger Suite
  - LigPrep
  - Protein Preparation Wizard
  - Glide

Ligand Preparation:
- DrugBank compounds (SDF format)
- OPLS force field
- pH = 7.0

Docking Workflow:
1. HTVS
2. SP Docking
3. XP Docking

Grid size: 20 Å in x, y, z
